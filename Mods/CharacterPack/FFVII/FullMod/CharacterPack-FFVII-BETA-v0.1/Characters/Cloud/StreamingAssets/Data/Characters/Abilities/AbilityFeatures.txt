# Handles the skill swap in Ex-SOLDIER
>AA 11068 ~~ Operator Mode Toggle ~~
[code=Patch] CheckAnyStatus(GetUnitProperty(50, 'CurrentStatus'), BattleStatus_CustomStatus26) ? 11068 : 11069 [/code]

>AA 11069 ~~ Punisher Mode Toggle ~~
[code=Patch] CheckAnyStatus(GetUnitProperty(50, 'CurrentStatus'), BattleStatus_CustomStatus26) ? 11068 : 11069 [/code]

>AA 11070 ~~ Counterstance ~~
[code=Disable] CasterCharacterIndex == 50 ? !CheckAnyStatus(GetUnitProperty(50, 'CurrentStatus'), BattleStatus_CustomStatus26) : 1 [/code]

>AA 11074 ~~ Prime Mode ~~
[code=Disable] CasterCharacterIndex == 50 ? !CheckAnyStatus(GetUnitProperty(50, 'CurrentStatus'), BattleStatus_CustomStatus26) : 1 [/code]

>CMD 4 ~~ Defend swaps to Counterstance when in Punisher Mode ~~
[code=Patch] CharacterIndex == 50 ? (CheckAnyStatus(CurrentStatus, BattleStatus_CustomStatus26) ? 11005 : 4) : -1 [/code]

>CMD 1 ~~ Attack swaps to Primed Attack when in Prime Mode ~~
[code=Patch] (CharacterIndex == 50 && CheckAnyStatus(CurrentStatus, BattleStatus_CustomStatus28)) ? 11006 : (HasSA(12071) ? 11003 : (HasSA(12070) ? 11002 : (HasSA(12069) ? 11001 : -1))) [/code]

>SA Global+ ~~ Primed Attack breaks damage limit ~~
Ability WhenBattleScriptStart EvenImmobilized
[code=Condition] AbilityId == 11075 [/code]
[code=CasterMaxDamageLimit] 10 * CasterMaxDamageLimit + 9 [/code]
Ability WhenEffectDone EvenImmobilized
[code=Condition] AbilityId == 11075 [/code]
[code=CasterMaxDamageLimit] CasterMaxDamageLimit / 10 [/code]

>SA Global+ ~~ Infinity's End breaks damage limit ~~
Ability WhenBattleScriptStart EvenImmobilized
[code=Condition] AbilityId == 11082 [/code]
[code=CasterMaxDamageLimit] 10 * CasterMaxDamageLimit + 9 [/code]
Ability WhenEffectDone EvenImmobilized
[code=Condition] AbilityId == 11082 [/code]
[code=CasterMaxDamageLimit] CasterMaxDamageLimit / 10 [/code]
