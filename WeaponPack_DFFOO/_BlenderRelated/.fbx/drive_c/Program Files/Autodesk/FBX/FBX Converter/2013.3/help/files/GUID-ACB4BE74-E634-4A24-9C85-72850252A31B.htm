Sharing camera position

 
 
 

You can share the position of the current camera.

3 shared Viewers A. Activate Camera and Share.

  1. Check the Share option on all FBX Viewers.
  2. Check the Camera option on every shared FBX Viewer.
NoteYou cannot control the position of an animated camera.
